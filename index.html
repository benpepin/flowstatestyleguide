Design Principles

The Flow State Principles system reflects the patterns and components that underpin the StreamLoan product.  These patterns and components provide a unified langauge and consistent look and feel when designing apps and products with the StreamLoan ecosystem.

We constantly keep these principles in mind when we design for StreamLoan.




Trustworthy


Be straightforward, transparent and authentic.  At all times work with user’s best interest in mind and build a sense of confidence in the app.


    • 1. Value users documents.
      2. Be transparent.
      3. Imagery, iconography and copy should demonstrate security concerns.

    • Supporting Characteristics: Secure, Stable, Clarity, Authenticity, Structured
